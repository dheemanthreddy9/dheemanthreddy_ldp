About Brown Bears

The brown bear (Ursus arctos) is native to parts of northern Eurasia and North America. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.

There are many subspecies including the Atlas bear and Himalayan brown bear.

Features

Brown bears have large curved claws, huge paws and can vary in color.

Quick Fact

Brown bears are found across much of North America and Eurasia.
